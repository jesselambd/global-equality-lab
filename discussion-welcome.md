# Welcome To Global Equality Lab

Welcome. This is a public strategy process for deciding what organization should be founded to reduce global economic inequality.

The starting question is simple:

Why should the country someone is born in determine what their work is worth?

The answer is not simple. Birthplace affects wages, opportunity, trust, legal rights, education, migration options, business networks, and access to customers. The lab exists because good intentions are not enough. We need to compare possible approaches and find one strong enough to build.

You are welcome here if you are a normal curious person, someone with lived experience, an expert, a builder, a funder, a critic, or an AI-assisted contributor.

Please use Discussions to:

- challenge a premise
- suggest a better approach
- submit evidence
- share lived experience
- point out risks or blind spots
- propose a pilot or implementation idea
- respond to another person's argument

Serious disagreement is welcome. The goal is not to protect one favorite idea. The goal is to find the strongest approach and use it as the foundation for a real organization.

If you are not sure where to begin, start with:

- What do you think the project is missing?
- What assumption seems wrong?
- What approach might work better?
- What would you test first?
