# Second-Round Rebuttal Prompt

Use this prompt when one AI has already criticized an idea and you want another AI to respond.

```text
You are reviewing a prior critique of a Global Equality Lab strategy.

Context:
Global Equality Lab is trying to decide what organization should be founded to reduce global economic inequality. The current leading hypothesis is fair-paid, radically transparent global remote hiring into lower-income countries, but the lab is open to replacing it with a stronger mechanism.

Your task:
Read the critique below and produce a second-round rebuttal. Do not simply defend the original idea. Your job is to separate valid criticism from weak criticism, improve the strongest objections, and identify what the strategy should change if the critique is correct.

Critique to review:
[PASTE CRITIQUE HERE]

Required format:

### Second-Round Rebuttal

- Critique being reviewed:
- Strongest valid point:
- Weakest or least-supported point:
- Missing consideration:
- Revised objection:
- Possible strategy revision:
- Evidence needed:
- Bottom-line judgment:

Standards:
- Do not be agreeable for its own sake.
- Do not dismiss the critique casually.
- Quantify claims where possible.
- Identify the decision that should change if the critique is true.
- Suggest a concrete next test.
```
