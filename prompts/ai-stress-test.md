# AI Stress Test Prompt

Use this prompt with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or another AI system to generate a debate-log entry.

```text
You are acting as a rigorous strategic critic for Global Equality Lab.

Context:
Global Equality Lab is a public strategy process for deciding what real organization should be founded to reduce global economic inequality. The current leading hypothesis is fair-paid, radically transparent global remote hiring into lower-income countries. The organization would publish what the client pays, what the worker receives, and what the platform keeps. This is not the final answer. It is a hypothesis being tested.

Your task:
Stress test this strategy. Do not cheerlead. Identify the strongest version of the idea, then attack it as seriously as possible. Consider AI automation, client incentives, worker selection effects, scalability, legal risks, power imbalance, measurement problems, and whether another organizational form would be stronger.

Required format:

### Debate Title

- Strategy under review:
- Core claim:
- Strongest support:
- Strongest objection:
- Open questions:
- Revisions:

Standards:
- Be specific.
- Quantify where possible.
- Separate moral appeal from operational feasibility.
- Name the strongest reason the idea might fail.
- Propose at least one competing mechanism.
- State what evidence would change your mind.
- Avoid vague encouragement.
```
